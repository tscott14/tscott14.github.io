A little more about me...

I began my journey in programming over a decade ago, inspired by the idea of modding the popular video game, Minecraft. This led me to delve into the world of software development, specifically in the Java programming language. Through hands-on experience, I obtained a natural intuition for the work, quickly aquiring the mindset of a programmer, laying a strong foundation for my future endeavors.

During my freshman year of high school, I enrolled in the only computer science class available, eventually progressing to an advanced placement course aimed at preparing students for the Collegeboard Advance Placement Computer Science test. Drawing from my earlier experiences, I excelled in this field, achieving top rankings in regional and state programming competitions and gaining extensive knowledge of the Java standard libraries. As a result, our year achieved the best performance in our high school's recorded history. With my high scores on both mock and actual AP exams, I became known as the go-to person for software development expertise.

My academic performance was strong enough that, when our professor was absent for a week, I was asked to assist the substitute in helping my classmates with the material and assignments, earning an honorary reward for my efforts. Those were rewarding experiences.

After high school, my interests expanded to include website development, graphics programming with OpenGL, and game engine development. It wasn't until a couple of years ago that I developed an interest in networking, leading me to pursue courses on network-centric processing in my university curriculum. These courses have had a significant impact on my understanding of how networks function and the various components that comprise them.

As I continue to navigate the exciting and ever-evolving world of technology and networking, I am eager to connect with individuals who share my passion and vision for the future. If you are willing to offer guidance, support, or collaboration in my journey to become a proficient leader in these fields, I wholeheartedly welcome your involvement. Your wisdom and mentorship would be invaluable as I strive to make a meaningful impact in the world of technology. Please don't hesitate to reach out and make your presence known - together, we can shape the future of this industry.
